Cheesy ham & broccoli pasta

Ingredients

300g pasta 1 head of broccoli 1 tbsp oil 1 onion 2 garlic cloves 250g ham 300ml pot double cream 1 tbsp English mustard 140g mature cheddar

Method

Bring a large pan of water to the boil and cook the pasta following pack instructions, adding the broccoli florets to the pan for the final 4 mins. Drain and set aside. Meanwhile, make the sauce. Heat the oil in a large pan and cook the onion for 5 mins to soften, then stir in the garlic and cook for 1 min more. Stir in the ham, cream and mustard, then bring to the boil. Add the pasta and broccoli, then stir in the cheese, coating everything in the sauce.
